We accept submissions exclusively from middle school, high school, and undergraduate students. The Journal welcomes all types of scholarly writing, including but not limited to research papers, review articles, and works in the humanities and social sciences. Issues are published monthly. Authors can generally expect to receive a response from the Journal within two to five weeks.

When submitting your paper, please ensure that it follows proper MLA citation guidelines, including in-text citations where applicable. Submissions should be provided as a link to a Google Document. The maximum length for submissions is 20 pages (1.15 line spacing, Times New Roman, font size 12), excluding the works cited section. We recommend reviewing the Example Article for detailed formatting guidance, as correctly formatted papers help streamline the publication process. After submission, each manuscript undergoes a peer-review process, during which two editors independently evaluate the paper to determine its suitability for publication. Every submission is carefully reviewed for academic integrity, factual accuracy, and grammatical precision. Once both editors reach their conclusions, they collaborate to decide on acceptance or rejection. We review submissions on a rolling basis, so authors are welcome to submit their papers at any time once they are complete.
